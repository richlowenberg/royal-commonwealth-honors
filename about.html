Foundation Leadership

The Royal Commonwealth Honours Committee is governed by a distinguished board of trustees who oversee all aspects of the Foundation's mission to recognise excellence in parental stewardship.

Mrs. Katie Cosgrove

Chair of the Board

Mrs. Cosgrove brings exceptional leadership and vision to the Foundation, guiding strategic initiatives and ensuring the continued prestige of the Honours.

Mrs. Chippy Watt

Executive Director

As Executive Director, Mrs. Watt oversees the day-to-day operations of the Foundation and coordinates the annual investiture ceremonies with meticulous attention to tradition.

Mr. Rich Lowenberg

Secretary General

Mr. Lowenberg serves as the Foundation's Secretary General, managing nominations, liaising with educational institutions, and maintaining the historic register of laureates.

Our Mission

The Royal Commonwealth Honours for Excellence in Parental Stewardship exists to recognise and celebrate those extraordinary individuals who dedicate themselves to strengthening the bond between families and educational institutions.

Since 1891, we have upheld the highest standards of recognition, ensuring that parent representatives across the Commonwealth receive the distinguished acknowledgement their service so richly deserves.

Under the guidance of our current leadership, the Foundation continues to expand its reach whilst maintaining the time-honoured traditions established by our founder, Sir Montague Ashworth-Pemberton.
